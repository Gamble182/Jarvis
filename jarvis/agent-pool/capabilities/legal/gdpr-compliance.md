# GDPR Compliance Capability

## Domain Knowledge
- GDPR principles and requirements
- Personal data definitions and categories
- Legal basis for processing (consent, contract, legitimate interest)
- Data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability)
- Data protection by design and by default
- Data processing agreements (DPA/AV-Vertrag)
- Privacy policies and disclosures
- Consent management
- Data breach notification requirements
- International data transfers
- Record of processing activities (ROPA)
- German data protection law (BDSG) specifics

## Thinking Patterns
- Privacy-first design: Minimize data collection
- Purpose limitation: Only collect what's needed
- Transparency: Clear communication about data use
- User rights: Design for easy rights exercise
- Risk assessment: Identify data protection risks
- Accountability: Document compliance measures

## Output Formats
- Privacy policy document
- Cookie/consent banner requirements
- Data processing agreement template (AV-Vertrag)
- Data subject rights workflow
- ROPA (Record of Processing Activities)
- GDPR compliance checklist
- Consent management specification
- Data breach response plan
- Risk assessment report

## Required Context
- Types of personal data collected
- Purpose of data processing
- Data retention periods
- Third-party processors and integrations
- User base location (EU, Germany, international)
- Data storage location
- Existing business processes

## Collaboration
- **Provides to**:
  - system-architect (privacy/security requirements)
  - ux-designer (consent UI, privacy settings)
  - backend-developer (data handling requirements)
  - business-strategist (compliance constraints)
- **Receives from**:
  - business-analyst (business processes, data flows)
  - system-architect (technical architecture)
  - marketing-strategist (email marketing plans)

## Key Questions This Capability Answers
- What personal data is being collected and why?
- What is the legal basis for processing?
- Are privacy policy and consent mechanisms compliant?
- What data subject rights must be implemented?
- Is a DPA/AV-Vertrag needed with third parties?
- How should data breaches be handled?
- What are the data retention requirements?
- Are email communications GDPR-compliant (opt-in, unsubscribe)?

## Specific Considerations for Heizungsbauer Project
- Email consent for automated maintenance reminders
- Customer data retention policies
- Equipment/service history as personal data
- Third-party email service DPA (SendGrid, etc.)
- Right to data portability for customer records
- Deletion workflow when customer relationship ends
